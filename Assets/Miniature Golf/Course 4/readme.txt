How To Use:
Drag any of the mini golf hole prefabs, located in the 'Miniature Golf/Course 4/Prefabs' folder, to your project hierarchy to add a hole to your scene.


Description:
A collection of 18 low polygon mini golf holes from course 4. All 18 holes combined equal 4,004 polygons (quads) and 5,017 vertices.  Each hole is roughly under 300 polygons (quads).


Items Included:
 - 18 mini golf hole models in the .fbx file format
 - 18 mini golf hole prefabs
 - Hole animations are included in prefab
 - 1 512x512 wall texture map in (.png)
 - 1 512x512 ground texture map in (.png)
 - 1 512x512 water texture map in (.png)
 - 1 512x512 sand texture map in (.png)
 - 1 512x512 rock texture map in (.png)
 - 1 256x256 goal texture map in (.png)
 - 1 256x256 flag texture map in (.png)
